Rooms SSajovic

A kindly welcome!


We have one double and one triple room, available for one or more nights. Organic
breakfast is on offer.

We are situated in a scenic location surrounded with greenery at a 5 minute walk to
Postojna caves and to the city centre. The house is adjacent to recreational
grounds (jogging track and football, basketball and tennis courts)

All rooms come with en-suite facilities, tea/coffee making and WIFI.

Triple/family room has also a separate entrance and a TV.

Private, free of charge and safe car park.
